## 1. Service Description

This application ("the App") is an automated content pipeline that creates and publishes educational video content about renewable energy and electricity tariffs in Spain. The App uses the TikTok Content Posting API to publish videos on behalf of authorized TikTok users.

## 2. User Authorization

- The App only publishes content to TikTok accounts that have explicitly authorized the App through TikTok's OAuth 2.0 flow.
- The authorized user (account owner) reviews and approves all content before it is published.
- No content is posted without the account owner's explicit approval.

## 3. Content

- All videos published through this App are educational in nature, covering topics such as electricity tariffs, solar energy, energy efficiency, and related subjects in Spain.
- Videos include an AI disclosure label (`is_aigc=true`) to indicate that AI tools were used in the creation process, in compliance with TikTok's policies.
- Content is based on publicly available data from official sources (CNMC, IDAE, Red Eléctrica, MITECO).

## 4. Data Collection

- The App collects only the minimum data required to publish content: TikTok OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens.
- No personal data beyond authorization tokens is collected, stored, or shared.
- See our [Privacy Policy](./privacy) for details.

## 5. Account Revocation

- Users can revoke the App's access at any time through TikTok's settings: **Settings → Security → Manage App Permissions**.
- Upon revocation, all stored tokens for that user are immediately invalidated.
