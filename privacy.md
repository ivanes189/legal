# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 3, 2026  
**App Name:** Content Posting API (EnergAI Content Factory)  
**Operator:** Ivan Salvado  

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the Content Posting API app ("the App") collects, uses, and protects information when you authorize the App to publish content on your TikTok account.

## 2. Data We Collect

The App collects only the following data:

| Data | Purpose | Retention |
|------|---------|-----------|
| TikTok OAuth access token | Authenticate API requests to publish videos | Until token expires or is revoked |
| TikTok OAuth refresh token | Renew expired access tokens | Until user revokes access |
| TikTok creator info (username, avatar URL) | Verify authorization and configure publishing settings | Duration of authorization |

**We do NOT collect:**
- Personal information (name, email, phone, address)
- TikTok profile data beyond what is listed above
- Browsing history or device information
- Content from your TikTok account (videos, comments, followers)

## 3. How We Use Your Data

- **Solely to publish content**: Authorization tokens are used exclusively to upload and publish educational videos to your TikTok account via the Content Posting API.
- **No other purpose**: We do not use your data for analytics, advertising, profiling, or any purpose other than content publishing.

## 4. Data Sharing

- **We do not share your data with any third parties.**
- Authorization tokens are stored securely and are never transmitted to anyone other than TikTok's API servers.

## 5. Data Storage and Security

- Tokens are stored in encrypted environment variables on secure servers.
- Access to tokens is restricted to the automated publishing pipeline.
- No tokens are logged, cached in plain text, or stored in source code.

## 6. Data Retention and Deletion

- Tokens are retained only while you have an active authorization with the App.
- When you revoke access (via TikTok Settings → Security → Manage App Permissions), all stored tokens become invalid and are deleted.
- You may also request deletion by contacting us at the email below.

## 7. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Revoke access** at any time through TikTok's app permissions settings.
- **Request deletion** of all stored data by contacting us.
- **Request information** about what data we hold about you.

## 8. Children's Privacy

This App does not knowingly collect data from children under 13 (or under 16 in the EU/EEA). The App is intended for use by adult content creators only.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Effective Date" above.
